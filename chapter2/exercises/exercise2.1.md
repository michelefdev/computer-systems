# Exercise 2.1 — Hex ↔ Binary Conversions

## Hex → Binary

1) 0x39A7F8

- Hex digits: `3 9 A 7 F 8`
- 4-bit binary: `0011 1001 1010 0111 1111 1000`
- Binary: `001110011010011111111000`

2) 0xD5E4C

- Hex digits: `D 5 E 4 C`
- 4-bit binary: `1101 0101 1110 0100 1100`
- Binary: `11010101111001001100`


## Binary → Hex

1) 1100100101111011

- Grouped nibbles: `1100 1001 0111 1011`
- Hex digits: `C 9 7 B`
- Hex: `0xC97B`

2) 1001101110011110110101

- Pad left and group: `0010 0110 1110 0111 1011 0101`
- Hex digits: `2 6 E 7 B 5`
- Hex: `0x26E7B5`
